History of My Little Bouquet

The idea for this project was born from a simple but powerful initiative: sending messages to retirement homes. Seeing the impact of these little attentions on residents, smiles, tears of joy, I realized the importance of offering a way to express one's feelings, even from a distance and in the most diverse situations. Thus, the platform offers a space where everyone can share messages of affection, support or simply a thought to those who matter, accompanying these words with a personalized bouquet. Whether it is to brighten the day of a loved one, bring a little warmth to isolated places like retirement homes, my site allows you to send flowers and words wherever the heart wishes.
